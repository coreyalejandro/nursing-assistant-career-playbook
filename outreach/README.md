# B2B Outreach Kit — Metro-Atlanta SNF Pilot

This folder is a **ready-to-send** outreach package for piloting the CNA Career
Playbook with skilled nursing facilities (SNFs), per the DeepSeek assessment's
recommendation to *"pilot B2B outreach to 3 Georgia SNFs with the ENTERPRISE.md
pitch."*

> **What this is and isn't.** This is a prepared campaign — a pitch, three tailored
> emails, and a tracker — built from **verified, sourced** facility research.
> Actually sending the emails / making the calls is a human action (it needs a real
> sender identity and inbox); it is intentionally **not** automated here. Fill in the
> `[Your …]` placeholders and send.

## Contents
| File | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `PITCH-ONEPAGER.md` | The core value proposition + honest scope + pilot structure. Shareable as-is. |
| `EMAILS.md` | Three cold emails, each tailored to a specific facility's verified situation. |
| `TRACKER.csv` | Pipeline tracker (status, dates, next action) pre-filled with the 3 targets. |

## The 3 verified targets (all CMS-certified as of May 2026 data)
1. **A.G. Rhodes at Wesley Woods** — Atlanta/DeKalb, independent nonprofit, 150+ beds. Angle: a *retention leader* (Newsweek #1 in GA; ~6-yr avg tenure) — the app reinforces an existing strength.
2. **Nurse Care of Buckhead** — Atlanta/Fulton, Wellington Healthcare, 220 beds, CMS 1-star. Angle: *staffing-pressure support* (retention is a Five-Star input) — framed respectfully.
3. **Briarwood Health Center by Harborview** — Tucker/DeKalb, Harborview (22-facility chain), 100 beds, CMS 3-star (4-star quality / 1-star staffing). Angle: *good care constrained by staffing*, plus multi-site upside.

## The anchor statistic (cite this)
- **~53.9%** average annual nursing-staff turnover across U.S. SNFs; **Georgia ~54.0%** — CMS turnover data compiled by CareListings (Mar 2023, `carelistings.com/statistics/snf-staff-turnover`); corroborated by the AHCA/NCAL 2023 workforce survey (Provider Magazine).
- These are **blended** nursing-staff figures (CNA + LPN + RN). CNA-only turnover is consistently the highest subcategory; the often-quoted "100%+" CNA figures come from older PHI/AHCA surveys and should be cited separately only if independently verified.

## Georgia certification context (for credibility on calls)
- Georgia CNAs must maintain active status on the **Georgia Nurse Aide Registry** (administered via the GA Dept. of Community Health / Secretary of State's licensing division) to work in a Medicare/Medicaid-certified facility. The app's certification module covers this renewal path. *(Confirm the current registry URL live before quoting it in writing.)*

## Honest scope reminder for every conversation
- **Live now:** the consumer app — free tier (10 AI chats/day) + Pro ($4.99/mo unlimited). No PHI by design.
- **Roadmap (designed, not built — see `../ENTERPRISE.md`):** enterprise tier with SSO, manager aggregate-only dashboards, audit logging, CMEK, and a signed BAA. Do not present these as available.

## Suggested workflow
1. For each facility, confirm the best recipient (administrator or DON) by phone.
2. Personalize the greeting in `EMAILS.md`, fill placeholders, send Tue–Thu AM.
3. Log the touch in `TRACKER.csv`; follow up once after ~5 business days.
4. On interest, send `PITCH-ONEPAGER.md` and book a 20-minute call.

*All facility facts trace to sources captured during research (CMS Care Compare / NursingHomeDatabase, the facilities' own sites, Newsweek). Items that could not be fully verified are flagged in the research notes rather than asserted.*
