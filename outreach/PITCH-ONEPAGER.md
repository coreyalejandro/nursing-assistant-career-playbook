## The problem you already feel

Nursing-staff turnover at U.S. skilled nursing facilities averages **~53.9% per year**, and Georgia sits right at the national average at **~54.0%** (CMS turnover data compiled by CareListings, March 2023; corroborated by the AHCA/NCAL 2023 workforce survey). CNAs — the hands-on backbone of resident care — churn fastest of all nursing roles. Every departure costs you overtime, agency premiums, onboarding time, and survey-affecting continuity of care. Staffing is also a direct input to your CMS Five-Star rating.

Most "career tools" ignore CNAs entirely: they're built for office workers, not for a workforce that is overwhelmingly women, disproportionately Black, Latina, and immigrant, working shifts on their feet.

## What we offer

A **vertical career companion built only for CNAs**, in English and Spanish, that helps your aides see a future *with* a path forward — which is what keeps people on the floor:

- **State-accurate certification & renewal guidance** — all 50 states + DC, including the Georgia Nurse Aide Registry renewal path, so aides keep credentials active.
- **The CNA → LPN/RN ladder**, mapped step by step with funding sources (e.g., HOPE Career Grant, TCSG programs).
- **Grounded wage data** anchored to U.S. BLS OEWS figures, plus a wage-negotiation coach.
- **Résumé tailoring + mock interviews** for internal advancement.
- **Trauma-informed burnout support** with a hard safety boundary: it is a *career* coach, refuses medical/clinical questions, redirects patient-safety issues to a supervising nurse, and surfaces the 988 crisis line. It never ingests protected health information (PHI is scrubbed and refused by design).
- **Works on any phone, offline-capable (PWA)** — meets your staff where they are.

## How a pilot works

1. **90-day no-cost pilot** for up to **25 of your CNAs** — we sponsor Pro access (normally $4.99/mo/user) for the pilot window.
2. We provide a one-page sign-up flyer (EN/ES) and a 15-minute virtual kickoff for your staff.
3. At day 90 we review simple, **privacy-safe** engagement signals together (sign-ups, active users, certification milestones reached) — no individual wellbeing or clinical data is ever shared with management.
