### Sending notes
- Best practice: send Tue–Thu morning; follow up once after 4–5 business days.
- For #2 and #3, a quick call to the published number to get the administrator's or DON's name (then personalizing the greeting) materially lifts reply rates.
- Keep attachments out of the first email; link to the live app instead (deliverability).
- Log every touch in `TRACKER.csv`.
